About

The project is being developed by the Associated Laboratory - Institute for Systems and Robotics (ISR-Lisboa). Our goal is to acquire, curate, and publicly release multi-modal datasets of different types of cancer to support and advance the research on personalized medicine for oncology patients.

Compromises

Our compromises are:

1.Curate and release new datasets;

2.Periodically updat and improve the existing datasets;

3.Provide benchmarks for different tasks using our datasets.This project proposes the development of a methodology for prognosis of ccRCC cancer using multimodality medical imaging and textual information.
